The product

Everything your team knows, in one place

Docs, wikis, projects, and databases that connect to each other, with AI woven through all of it.

📝

Docs

Flexible, block-based documents for anything from a quick note to a full spec. Real-time collaboration, comments, and version history built in.

📚

Wikis

Organize company knowledge so nothing gets lost. Verify pages, set owners, and make one source of truth your whole team trusts.

✅

Projects

Plan and track work with boards, timelines, and tables. Link every task to the docs and context behind it.

📊

Databases

The engine underneath Foldspace. Turn any collection into a database with custom properties, filters, relations, and rollups.

📅

Calendar

Foldspace Calendar connects your schedule to your work, so deadlines, docs, and meetings live in one timeline.

🌐

Sites

Publish any Foldspace page as a fast, shareable website. Great for docs, wikis, and lightweight landing pages.

🔁

Integrations & API

Connect Slack, GitHub, Figma, and more, or build your own with the Foldspace API. Your workspace fits into your stack.

📁

Templates

Thousands of ready-made setups for meeting notes, CRMs, roadmaps, and habit trackers. Duplicate and customize in seconds.

Foldspace AI

AI that works where your knowledge lives

Ask a question and Foldspace AI answers using your own docs, wikis, and projects, with sources. Draft, rewrite, translate, and summarize inline. Autofill database properties from context. Because it is built into the workspace, it already has the context that standalone chatbots lack.

  • Answers from your workspace, with citations
  • Inline drafting, editing, and summarizing
  • Connected search across Slack, Drive, and more
