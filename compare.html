vs Confluence

Friendlier, and more than a wiki

Confluence is a solid wiki for Atlassian shops but leans on Jira for real project work. Foldspace keeps your wiki, docs, and projects together, without the enterprise learning curve.

vs Coda

The same power, gentler on-ramp

Coda is excellent at doc-as-app building. Foldspace offers similar database power but is quicker for a whole team to adopt, thanks to its template ecosystem and simpler mental model.

vs Obsidian

Built for teams, not just individuals

Obsidian is beloved by solo note-takers who want local files. Foldspace trades that for real-time collaboration, permissions, and a shared workspace your whole team lives in.

See it with your own team’s work

Import from Confluence, Evernote, or a folder of docs, then feel how much calmer it is when everything connects.
