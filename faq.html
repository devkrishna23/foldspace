Questions, answered

Frequently asked questions

Straight answers to what people ask before adopting Foldspace, including the common myths.

Getting started

Isn’t Foldspace too complicated to learn?

It is more flexible than most tools, which can feel like a lot on day one, but you do not have to build anything from scratch. Start from a template for meeting notes, a wiki, or a project tracker and you are productive in an afternoon. Most teams find the flexibility becomes the reason they stay, not the reason they leave.

Is Foldspace just a note-taking app?

No. Notes are one small part. Foldspace combines docs, team wikis, project management, and flexible databases in one workspace, with AI built in. Many teams run their entire company knowledge base, roadmap, and CRM inside it.

Do I have to build my whole workspace myself?

Not unless you want to. There are thousands of templates in the gallery for almost every use case, and you can duplicate one and start using it immediately, then customize as your needs evolve.

Using it for real work

Is Foldspace good for real project management?

Yes. Projects can be viewed as boards, timelines, tables, or calendars, with custom properties, filters, and relations. Because tasks live next to the docs and specs behind them, context does not get lost the way it does when your tracker and your docs are separate tools.

Does Foldspace get slow with large workspaces?

Performance has improved substantially, and most teams run large wikis and databases without issue. For very large databases, using filtered views and linked databases keeps things fast, rather than loading thousands of rows at once.

Does Foldspace work offline?

Foldspace is primarily a real-time, cloud-based workspace, and it offers limited offline access for pages you have recently opened. If fully offline, local-first editing is essential to you, that is worth testing for your workflow before committing.

AI, data, and security

How does Foldspace AI use my data?

Foldspace AI answers using the content in your own workspace and cites the pages it drew from. Your workspace data is not used to train the underlying models, and AI features can be managed by admins at the workspace level.

Is my company data secure?

Foldspace supports SAML single sign-on, granular permissions, private team spaces, audit logs on higher tiers, and encryption in transit and at rest. Enterprise plans add advanced controls like user provisioning and extended admin tooling.

Can I import from Confluence, Evernote, or Google Docs?

Yes. Foldspace has importers for Confluence, Evernote, Word, and more, plus copy-paste that preserves formatting. Most teams migrate their existing docs in a single sitting.

What does it cost?

Foldspace is free for individuals. Paid team plans start at $10 per seat per month for Plus and $20 per seat per month for Business, which includes Foldspace AI. Enterprise pricing is custom. All paid plans offer a trial.
